## 📱 About

**Mog.AI** is an all-in-one AI-powered fitness companion that combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with proven fitness science. It delivers personalized training programs, nutrition tracking, body scanning, and 24/7 AI coaching — all in one powerful mobile app.

> Built with React Native, TypeScript, and advanced AI/ML pipelines.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🤖 AI Body Scanning
Snap a photo and get an instant AI-powered physique analysis. Track muscle development, body composition, and progress over time with detailed breakdowns.

### 💪 Personalized Workout Plans
Get custom 4-week training programs generated by AI based on your body analysis, goals, and experience level. Every workout is tailored to you.

### 🍎 Smart Nutrition Tracking
Scan your meals and instantly log calories and macros. Stay on top of your nutrition with AI-powered food recognition.

### 📊 Advanced Analytics
Dive deep into your progress with detailed charts, muscle group breakdowns, and trend tracking. See exactly how your body is changing.

### 🏆 Leaderboard & Community
Compete with others, climb the rankings, and stay motivated with a community of like-minded fitness enthusiasts.

### 💬 AI Coach
Chat with your AI fitness assistant for workout tips, form advice, nutrition guidance, and motivation — available 24/7.

## 🚀 How It Works

1. **Tell Us About You** — Complete a quick onboarding to share your goals, fitness level, dietary preferences, and available equipment.
2. **Get Your AI Plan** — Our AI instantly creates a personalized training and nutrition program tailored specifically to you.
3. **Train & Transform** — Follow your plan, track progress, and watch as the AI continuously adapts to keep you progressing.
